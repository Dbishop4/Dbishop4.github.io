Case Studies

Cyclistic Bike-Share Analysis

This project analyzed Cyclistic’s bike-share data to uncover user behavior trends and create a marketing strategy to convert casual riders into annual members. Tackling a dataset of over 5 million rows locally, utilizing R and its powerful libraries to clean, analyze, and visualize key insights.

  • Skills Highlighted: Data Cleaning, Visualization, Statistical Analysis
  • Key Insights:
    • Casual riders favor weekends for longer, leisure-oriented rides.
    • Members consistently ride on weekdays, aligning with commuter patterns.
    • Electric bikes are significantly preferred by casual riders, presenting a targeted marketing opportunity.
  • Tools Used: R, ggplot2, data.table, tidyverse
  • Results and Recomendations:
    • Offer a weekend unlimited membership: Casual riders show a strong preference for weekend activity.
      Introducing a targeted weekend membership plan could incentivize casual users to commit to the service and increase retention.
    • Explore referral credits for existing members: Incentivizing current members with credits or discounts for successful referrals could help grow the customer base and promote member loyalty.
    • Focus on electric bike promotions: Highlighting electric bike usage in campaigns aimed at casual riders could capitalize on their existing preference for these rides and encourage further engagement.
